Welcome to Indian Chamber of Commerce in Switzerland

Bridging Business, Trade and Investment Between India and Switzerland

The Indian Chamber of Commerce in Switzerland (ICC Switzerland) is a premier platform dedicated to strengthening economic and business ties between India and Switzerland. Our mission is to empower businesses, facilitate bilateral trade, and promote investment opportunities by leveraging a robust network of professionals, industry leaders, and policymakers.

Why Partner with ICC Switzerland?

Tailored Business Support: We offer customised services to help Indian and Swiss businesses navigate market entry, expansion strategies, and business development opportunities. Strategic Networking: Join an exclusive community of like-minded professionals and connect with key stakeholders from various industries at our high-impact networking events. Market Intelligence & Insights: Gain access to valuable resources, industry research, and expert insights on the Swiss and Indian business landscapes.

Our Services

  • Strategic Business Support: Comprehensive advisory services designed for Indian and Swiss companies looking to enter new markets, expand operations, or enhance business development strategies. Our expertise extends to legal compliance, taxation, and regulatory frameworks, ensuring seamless and compliant business operations.

  • High-Value Networking: Gain exclusive access to a distinguished network of industry leaders, investors, and key decision-makers through our networking events in both India and Switzerland, fostering strategic collaborations and growth opportunities.

  • Market Intelligence & Insights: Leverage in-depth research, industry trend analysis, and expert reports on digital transformation, economic developments, and market dynamics, providing actionable intelligence to navigate the Swiss and Indian business landscapes effectively.

For further information, please contact our Chief Representative Nayan Srivastava, Email: icc.switzerland@indianchamber.net or our Deputy Chief Representative, Chrys Kamber Email: icc.switzerland1@indianchamber.net

Become a member of ICC Switzerland to expand your business, access new opportunities, and be part of a thriving business ecosystem that bridges India and Switzerland.
